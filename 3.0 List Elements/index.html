Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the warm milk with the yeast and sugar in a bowl. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the egg, egg yolk, melted butter, and the yeast mixture.
  3. Add the flour gradually, mixing until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes until smooth.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for 2 hours or until doubled in size.
  6. Roll out the dough into a large rectangle on a floured surface.
  7. For the filling, melt the butter and mix it with the sugars and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the filling evenly over the rolled-out dough.
  9. Roll the dough tightly into a log and cut it into 3cm sections.
  10. Place the rolls flat in a greased baking tray.
  11. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and bake for 20-25 minutes until lightly golden brown.
